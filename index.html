The Trials of Kathran'dur

A D&D 5e One-shot adventure for parties of 4-6 at levels 10-13

A TR Grimm adventure

This adventure article uses the full array of offered materials from Wizards of the Coast pulling most heavily from: Volo's Guide to Monsters (from here forward referred to as VGM); Xanathar’s Guide to Everything (to be referred to as XGE). Unlike many modules and adventure guides this article can also be used as an overarching adventure setting with the stalwart City in the Mountain and its varied and admittedly… eccentric inhabitants acting as a colorful, rich and dangerous background to parties of any size and power level. With that being said this is to act as an introduction to the City State of Kathran’dure, the Mercentocrocy, The Mountain Guard and the Rouge’s Court as well as other important and notable individuals that claim the Kathran Ranges and Kathran’dure itself home.
